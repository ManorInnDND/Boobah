Broglyn Quests

Quests from Broglyn...

Bash Some Skulls

This is the primary quest Broglyn will send out. It is imperative that the party redeem themselves by beating down some half-orc scoundrels who ripped him off. The party will need to venture to the fifth below and deal with the thugs.

Trojan Dragon

The dragon grafters the party killed on the mission into the abandoned mine with the bio lab are causing more trouble. A group of punk rock grafters led by a blue dragon grafted ogre named Cold Horns have let slip their wherabouts on Broglyn's level of the undercity (The Fifth Below). The party is to go to their hideout and kill them all, they can go there and kill them outright or meet with a lab tech, Grufly Stonebladder who claims to have an elegant solution.

Lurkwood Silvered Weapons Delivery

The Grey Wolves are a tribe of Uthgartd that live in the Lurkwood, Northeast of Mirabar by a day and one half. One of Broglyns pals has a mine just inside the forest that has come under attack from the Grey Wolves. He wants a delivery of silvered axes to fend off the wolves.

Melt the Chill

A nonhuman mercenary group known as The Chill have a tower in the Lurkwood. Broglyn hired them to harvest and deliver a crate of redcap mushrooms but they double crossed him and sold directly to his buyer. He wants the party to head to the bar they hang at and beat them down, no killing, at least not everyone. The hang on the third below in a dive bar.

Time to Sleep

Go to the Lurkwood and steal/murder a tibe of wood elves who posses a sleep inducing salve known as headrar, watch out for the Thunderbeast Tribe.

Other Quests

There are other places you might find work in Mirabar...

Temple District

Chauntea and Tymora are main deities, Chauntea is earth mother. She represents abundance and agriculture. There is rumored to be a massive garden in town that her preists work. Tymora is a godess of luck.

Mercenary Guilds

Guard deep mines, slay beasts... who knows?
